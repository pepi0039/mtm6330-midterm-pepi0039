ABOUT

Hey! I'm a creative person based out of Ottawa, Ontario. I love simple design, clean lines and working with some retro aesthetics tossed in for good measure. I'm currently studying at Algonquin College's School of Media and Design. Outside of school, you can find me taking care of plants, obsessing over Scandinavian furniture and taking photos.
